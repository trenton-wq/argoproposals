What You Told Us
Five things that actually move the needle.
We scrapped the first proposal and rebuilt it around what you said matters. No filler. No bloat.

Recruiters Can't Take Vacation

When a recruiter steps away, they still feel like they have to stay "on." Candidates need updates, clients expect responsiveness. You need an AI agent under each recruiter that keeps communication, follow-ups, and status updates moving while they actually disconnect.

Increase Recruiter Capacity

Each recruiter works 10-12 job orders. If AI handles the busy work (write-ups, submissions, follow-up), that capacity grows to 13-15. More capacity per recruiter means more revenue for the business and more earnings for the team. Everyone wins.

Sourcing Needs to Run in the Background

Bo's dream: an AI agent crawling boards, messaging candidates, and feeding warm responses with booked calls directly to recruiters. Not replacing sourcing, but amplifying it so the team gets qualified people reaching out to them.

Write-Ups Eat Recruiter Hours

Submissions pile up fast. Recruiters spend hours each week writing them up when they should be talking to candidates. An agent trained in your team's voice could draft submissions and follow-ups, giving recruiters that time back to work more orders.

Warm Leads from Content

You know how to close. You just need more at-bats. Content that positions Bronwick as the category leader in stone industry recruiting is the most efficient way to generate warm inbound for both candidates and clients.

The Approach
Brick by brick. One workflow at a time.
We start with the highest-impact workflow. Prove it works. Then stack the next one on top. Here's the order we'd recommend.
01

Recruiting Agent: Submissions & Follow-Up

We build an AI agent trained on your team's voice and style that drafts candidate submissions, write-ups, and follow-up communications. Human oversight catches anything the agent misses before it goes out. This is the workflow that frees up the most recruiter time immediately.

Measure: Hours saved per recruiter per week · Additional job orders worked
02

Vacation Coverage Agent

Now that the recruiting agent has been built, vacation coverage is ready. Each recruiter gets an AI agent that handles candidate communication, status updates, and scheduling while they're out. A part-time Argo operator monitors the agents to make sure nothing falls through. Recruiters come back to a warm pipeline instead of a cold one.

Measure: Pipeline activity maintained during time off · Candidate NPS
03

Sourcing Agent

An always-on agent that crawls job boards and candidate databases, identifies potential matches for active job orders, and sends outreach on behalf of your recruiters. Warm responses get booked directly on the recruiter's calendar. They wake up with candidates ready to talk.

Measure: Qualified candidates sourced per job order · Response rate
04

Content & Social Selling Engine

Content for Bo's personal brand plus recruiter-specific social selling for each team member. Installers see Zane talking shop floor language. Sales managers see your senior recruiters speaking their world. The team becomes visible to the people they recruit and sell to, generating warm inbound from both sides.

Measure: Warm inbound leads per month · Impressions · Engagement
