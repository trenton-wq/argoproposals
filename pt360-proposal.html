The Reality
PT recruitment is broken. You already know this.
The PT labor market is tight and getting tighter. Traditional recruiting methods are either too expensive, too slow, or both.

Traditional Recruiters Are Expensive

PT staffing agencies charge 20–25% of first-year salary. For a PT making $80K, that's $16,000–$20,000 per hire. Multiply that by three and you're looking at $50K+.

Job Boards Are a Black Hole

Post and pray doesn't work for PT roles. The best candidates aren't scrolling Indeed — they're being reached directly by people who know where to find them.

Every Open Chair Costs You

An unfilled PT position means lost revenue, strained staff, and patients who can't get seen. The longer it takes, the more it costs in every direction.

You Don't Have Time for This

You're running a practice, not a recruiting department. Sourcing, outreach, screening, and follow-up shouldn't be on your plate.
